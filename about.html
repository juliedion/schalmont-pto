ℹ️ About the Schalmont PTO

We are a community of parents, guardians, and educators working together to support every student in the Schalmont Central School District.

Our Mission

Building Community,
Enriching Education

The Schalmont Central PTO exists to connect and unite the parent-teacher organizations of Woestina Pre-K, Jefferson Elementary, Schalmont Middle School, and Schalmont High School — strengthening the bond between home and school across our entire district.

Through fundraising, family events, and direct volunteer support, we work hand-in-hand with teachers and school administration to create an exceptional educational experience for all Sabres.
