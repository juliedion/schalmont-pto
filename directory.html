📋 Parent Directory

Connect with other Schalmont families. Add your contact information to make it easy for other parents and guardians to reach you.

Join the Directory

Why does this exist? Schools are not permitted to release contact information for other children or families. This directory was created because parents repeatedly struggled to gather information for things like birthday party invitations, playdates, and carpools. Joining is completely optional — only share what you're comfortable with.
📷

Children and students will be visible to other Schalmont families. Add each child's name, grade, school, teacher, your relationship to them, and optionally a second parent or guardian.

What to display in the directory:

📱 PTO Text Reminders

Opt in to receive occasional text reminders about PTO events, meetings, and announcements.

🔒 Privacy Notice: Your information is only visible to other registered Schalmont PTO families. You control what contact details are shown. Your email is used only for PTO communications.
