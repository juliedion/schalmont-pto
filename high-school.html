ℹ️ About the Schalmont High School PTO

The Schalmont Central PTO supports High School students and families through events, fundraisers, and programs that celebrate student achievement and enrich the high school experience. We work in partnership with staff and administration to create a supportive community for every Sabre through graduation and beyond.

Our board members are here to serve the Schalmont High School community and welcome your involvement.
