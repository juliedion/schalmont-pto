ℹ️ About the Jefferson Elementary PTO

The Jefferson Elementary PTO is a dedicated group of parents, teachers, and community members working together to enrich the educational experience for all students at Jefferson Elementary School. We organize events, fundraisers, and programs that build connections between families, staff, and the broader Schalmont community.

Our board members are here to serve Jefferson families and welcome your ideas, feedback, and involvement.

Supporting students, connecting families, and building community in the Schalmont Central School District.
