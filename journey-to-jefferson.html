About Journey to Jefferson

Journey to Jefferson is a special transition program designed to welcome Pre-K and incoming Kindergarten families to Jefferson Elementary School. This program helps students and families feel comfortable, confident, and excited about the next step in their educational journey.

Have questions about Journey to Jefferson or the transition to Jefferson Elementary? Reach out to our Jefferson PTO President, Corrie Hoffman-Haddix, for more information.
