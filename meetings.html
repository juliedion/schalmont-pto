📋 PTO Meetings

All Schalmont parents and guardians are welcome at our monthly PTO meetings. Come share ideas, hear updates, and help shape our community.

Meeting Schedule

Meetings are held monthly during the school year.

Everyone Is Welcome

You don't need to be a board member or even a member to attend — PTO meetings are open to all Schalmont parents and guardians.

Meetings cover upcoming events, fundraising updates, school news, and open discussion. Your voice matters!
