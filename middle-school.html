ℹ️ About the Schalmont Middle School PTO

The Schalmont Central PTO supports Schalmont Middle School students and families through events, fundraisers, and programs that enrich the middle school experience. We work in partnership with staff and administration to create a welcoming and supportive community for every Sabre.

Our board members are here to serve the Schalmont Middle School community and welcome your involvement.
