🌟 Staff Appreciation Week

May 4–8, 2026 · Thank you to our incredible staff!

Woestina

🌱 Woestina Pre-K Appreciation

We are so grateful for the teachers, aides, and staff at Woestina who give so much to our youngest learners every day. During Staff Appreciation Week (May 4–8), show them some love!

🥔
Outback Steakhouse Loaded Baked Potato Bar
Wednesday, May 6

Outback Steakhouse will be donating a fully loaded baked potato bar for our Woestina staff. A delicious treat to say thank you!

For questions about Woestina Staff Appreciation Week activities, please contact Corrie Haddix at corrie@jeffersonpto.com.

Jefferson Elementary

🏫 Jefferson Elementary Appreciation

Our Jefferson teachers and staff work tirelessly to create a nurturing, fun learning environment. Let's show them how much we appreciate everything they do during Teacher Appreciation Week, May 4–8!

🥔
Outback Steakhouse Loaded Baked Potato Bar
Wednesday, May 6

Outback Steakhouse will be donating a fully loaded baked potato bar for our Jefferson staff. A delicious thank you to our incredible team!

For questions about Jefferson Staff Appreciation Week activities, please contact Corrie Haddix at corrie@jeffersonpto.com.
